Lasagna

Desccription

Lasagna (plural "asagne") is a flat expanded pasta sheet traditionally made in italy with Parmesan cheese white sauce, a meat based sauce. Despite the traditionall form still being popular, lasagna has taken many different recepise over the years.

Lasagna is a rich and creamy whole meat-meat pasta dish filed layer by layer with refreshingly fresh onions and garlic, lathered in a succulent auce and topped with imported, premium quality mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sacue
  2. Coock the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta micture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recepie instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
